Predict

Every price is tradable.

Most prediction markets ask a single yes-or-no question, like whether Bitcoin closes above $100,000 by December, and most of that menu has already settled at zero or one hundred. Predict quotes any level, like 94,820 right now, in any direction, across timeframes from one minute to a month. A single pool stands behind every quote, which is how the whole range stays open.

01

Any price level

Continuous pricing across the range, so the levels worth trading stay open instead of sitting at zero or one hundred.

02

Range markets

Take a position on where the price lands inside a band, or on either direction out of it.

03

Leverage in the contract

Financing sits inside the position itself. No margin call, no debt, and the downside stops at zero.

04

One pool behind every quote

A single pool is counterparty to every trade. The nearest traditional equivalent is a custom quote from a desk, priced automatically onchain.

Spot

One book. Every venue on Sui.

Orders route through the same order book that powers trading across the Sui ecosystem. Depth compounds instead of fragmenting, so the book you trade on gets deeper as the ecosystem grows.
